Philosophy & services

IO takes a holistic approach to building your brand and organization.


To build a brand, you need a distinctive identity and processes that empower your team to uphold your brand promise day by day. To exceed expectations and strengthen brand loyalty, you need to create memorable moments.

BRANDING

1/3

In an overwhelmingly crowded world, it's increasingly hard to make a good first impression or any impression at all. That's why you need a memorable brand identity that inspires the right crowd. A beloved brand evokes positive emotions and drives preferred behaviors.

The process of creating such a brand is challenging because you need to blend both emotional and logical components to build it successfully.

We guide you through this process using proven branding frameworks, designed to unearth the insights that can inspire your new brand identity and fuel the connection it needs to establish with your employees or customers.

Once we build your new identity, we need to implement it through every touchpoint a company has with employees or customers, from advertising to services. Every interaction builds a brand, so every touchpoint is equally important.

PEOPLE & ORGANIZATIONAL DEVELOPMENT

2/3

To be able to make your new brand a reality, you need a well-oiled machine that builds it. That machine is your organization. We design and optimize your internal processes leveraging the principles of service design and organizational design, so you can live up to the promise your brand gives to your employees and customers.

We work closely with you to diagnose all the communication blockers and organizational pain points, then use our methodology to rebuild and nurture your new processes.

New processes have to empower your people, before they empower your business. Even though we invest a lot of time in understanding your business, our focus always remains on people. We believe that a sincere conversation yields more helpful insights than all the data you hold on your servers.

BIG MOMENTS

3/3

Once your organization starts working towards keeping your brand promise, it's time to exceed the expectations.

Research has found that when we remember an experience, we ignore most of what happened and focus instead on a few particular moments. We call them Big moments, because they are so meaningful our brain makes a special record of them. Big moments can energize us, empower us, change us.

For example, a new employee comes to your organization. Your employer brand reputation motivated them to apply. Your flawless onboarding processes made them feel at home from the get-go. But that moment when their team lead took them to lunch unexpectedly, to celebrate their first month at the company will be etched in their memory forever, boosting their loyalty. That's what big moments do.

Luxury retail and hospitality industry need a big moment strategy to make a lasting impression and differentiate themselves from competition in oversaturated markets. We map out carefully choreographed big moments scenarios, connecting emotions and memories to online and offline spaces.

Business bonds are built on trust and reliability, but are also strengthened by special events and gifts that mark important milestones. A corporate gift can be an item randomly selected from a catalogue, but it can also be a carefully crafted big moment that can fuel loyalty and open doors to new opportunities.
